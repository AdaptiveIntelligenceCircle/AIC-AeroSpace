## Our Commitment

We commit to building an open, respectful, and ethically grounded research community.

Space is not an escape from responsibility.
It amplifies responsibility.

## Expected Behavior

- Act with intellectual honesty
- Respect diverse scientific, cultural, and philosophical perspectives
- Prioritize safety, ethics, and long-term consequences
- Engage in constructive and evidence-based discussions

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Militarization advocacy
- Exploitative or extractive ideology toward space resources
- Harassment, discrimination, or intimidation
- Misrepresentation of research results for hype or funding

## Scientific Integrity

All contributions must:
- Clearly state assumptions and limitations
- Avoid misleading benchmarks or exaggerated claims
- Distinguish speculation from validated research

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may:
- Remove content that violates this code
- Restrict participation when necessary
- Prioritize project ethics over popularity or growth

## Responsibility

Participation implies acceptance that:
**Intelligence beyond Earth affects all humanity.**

We choose caution, humility, and foresight.
